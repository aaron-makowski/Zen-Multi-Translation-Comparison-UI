# Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering an open and welcoming environment. By participating in this project you agree to abide by the following guidelines:

- Be respectful and considerate of others.
- Provide constructive feedback and welcome different viewpoints.
- Use inclusive language and avoid personal attacks.
- Follow the project's contribution guidelines and maintainers' decisions.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.
